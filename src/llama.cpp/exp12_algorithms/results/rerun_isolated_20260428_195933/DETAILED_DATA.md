## Notes

- 50% scheduling load has high variance: some runs remain around 30 tok/s, while some drop to about 3 tok/s. The mean speedup is therefore useful but unstable.
- 80-100% load saturates the shared load/decode cores. In that regime, neither scheduling nor timeout policy reliably improves throughput.
- The timeout quality columns are only lightweight next-token proxies, not a full perplexity or downstream accuracy evaluation.
